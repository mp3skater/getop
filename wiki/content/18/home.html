Get Painite

First step is to get the new ore: Painite. It can be found in The End on the bottom of the floating islands. With painite you can activate the weapons, get a super OP set of armor and more. See the video below to see a way you can get 'em.

Get the Weapons

The weopons are the hearth of the mod. You can find them in the new structures across all 3 standard dimensions (Overworld, The Nether, The End). Though stay allert, some of them are guarded with mobs. The whole list of weapons and where you can find them is listed in the {LINK}weapons and {LINK}structures page from the menu. Activate the Weapons

To activate the weapons you need to craft it using the Anvil of Sage(see crafting recipe below). Then insert the broken weapon and the painite ingot(recipe also below) in the apposite slots(see below). That activates your weapon and it is now ready to be used, once its durability goes to 0 the weapons is broken again, to prevent that you can "heal" it in the normal anvil using painite or use mending.
